Want to Contribute?

We're always looking for new tiny art! Whether you're a local artist, student, teacher, or someone else, we'd love to feature your miniature creations. All ages and skill levels are welcome.

Guidelines: Here’s what to know before submitting:

  • Most pieces are 4" × 4" or smaller (any size is fine as long as it fits).
  • All mediums welcome: painting, drawing, sculpture, origami, collage, poetry, and more.
  • If you're unsure whether your piece works, just ask! We're open to almost anything that fits.

The internal gallery dimensions are 11"W × 23"L × 16"H.
